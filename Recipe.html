  1. About the Recipe

    2. Veg Momos, a steamed or fried dumpling stuffed with mixed vegetables, is a delicious snack food enjoyed in Tibet region. From last few years, Momos are getting very popular in other parts of India and you can easily prepare them at home by either steaming or deep frying.Steps to cook Vegetarian Momos at home can be classified in three parts:

    1. prepare fresh vegetable stuffing
    2. bind the dough
    3. fill rolled dough with mixed vegetable mixture and give them a “Potli” shape
    4. Steam cook them.

    This Veg Momos recipe explains all three parts with step by step photos.

  2. Preparation and Cooking Timings

    1. Preparation Time: 1hr
    2. Cooking Time :20 min
    3. Serves: 2 people

  3. Ingredients Required

    4. Ingredients for Dough
    3/4 cup all purpose flour
    1 teaspoon oil
    salt to taste

    Ingredients For stuffing
    1 tablespoon Oil 3-4 cloves of Garlic, finely chopped 1 teaspoon finely chopped Ginger 1/2 cup finely chopped Carrot
    1/2 cup finely chopped Cabbage 1/4 cup finely chopped Capsicum 1/4 cup finely chopped Green French Beans (fanasi) 1/4 cup finely chopped Green Onion or Onion
    ½-1 teaspoon Soy Sauce 1 teaspoon Chilli Sauce 1/2 teaspoon Black Pepper Powder Salt to taste

  4. Step by Step process

    5. Make The Dough

    1. Take 3/4 cup all-purpose flour, ¼ teaspoon salt and 1 teaspoon oil in a bowl. Mix well with a spoon.



    2. Add 2 to 3 tablespoon water in parts and begin to knead to a firm dough. Do not make the dough soft as then it becomes difficult to shape the dumplings. If required you can add 1 to 2 tablespoons more water.

    3. Cover the dough with a moist napkin or kitchen towel. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes.

    Make Vegetable Stuffing

    1. Finely chop the vegetables. You will need 1.5 to 1.75 cups of finely chopped veggies. You can also use a food processor to finely chop vegetables like cabbage, carrots, french beans.


    2. Heat oil in a thick bottomed frying pan or wok. Add ½ teaspoon finely chopped garlic. Sauté for 2 to 3 seconds on medium-low heat.


    3. Add all the finely chopped vegetables.Increase the flame and stir fry the vegetables on a medium to high heat. If you have not used a thick bottomed pan, then sauté the veggies on a low to medium heat.



    4. Then add 1 teaspoon soy sauce, ½ teaspoon black pepper and salt as per taste.


    5. The vegetable stuffing is ready. Let the stuffing cool completely.

    Portion and Roll Dough

    1. Divide the dough in two parts.Make a 7 to 8 inches log from each part. cut the log into equal slices.


    2. Take each dough ball and on a lightly dusted board. Roll each dough ball into a thin circle of about 3 to 4 inches diameter.

    Steaming Veg Momos

    1. Grease a steamer pan with some oil.Arrange them in the pan, keeping space between them



    2. Cover and steam for 5 to 6 minutes till the outer dough becomes transparent. Donot overcook as then the outer dough, becomes dense and chewy. The steaming time may vary upon the thickness of momos cover.
    3. Serve veg momos hot with momos chutney or red chilli-garlic chutney, spicy schezwan sauce or tomato sauce. They also go best with a spicy sauce and should be eaten hot.

  5. Expert's Tip

    • Dough: The dough has to be firm and tight. Do not add more water and make the dough soft. A softer dough won’t be easy to shape.

    • Stuffing: For the stuffing you could use your favorite veggies. You could also make a stuffing with just button mushrooms or a mix of fresh mushrooms. Another variation would be use some mashed or boiled potatoes and paneer (Indian cottage cheese).

    • Spices, herbs and seasonings: In the stuffing I prefer some umami and so I love adding soy sauce. But you can experiment with different flavors here. Say Indian ground spices and herbs, Mexican seasoning, Miso or any flavor profile you like.
